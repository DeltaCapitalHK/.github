# Code of Conduct

We expect respectful, professional, and lawful communication across Delta&Capital public repositories.

Do not use issues, discussions, or pull requests to publish:

- Personal attacks or harassment
- Private client information
- Confidential case materials
- Wallet access credentials
- Exchange account materials
- Misleading recovery claims
- Spam or impersonation content

We may remove content or restrict participation when necessary to protect users, clients, and the integrity of public resources.
