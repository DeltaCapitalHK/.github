# Delta&Capital (德尔泰)

**Digital asset incident response, blockchain forensic analysis, exchange account review support, and recovery coordination.**

Delta&Capital (德尔泰) supports individuals, institutions, and professional advisors in understanding digital asset incidents, organizing on-chain evidence, preparing structured case materials, and coordinating documentation-based workflows for compliance, legal, exchange review, and recovery-related matters.

We focus on practical case support across blockchain transaction tracing, wallet risk review, exchange account restrictions, evidence preservation, and digital asset incident documentation.

## Focus Areas

- Digital asset incident response
- Blockchain transaction tracing
- Wallet and counterparty risk review
- CEX/DEX account restriction and risk-control review support
- Documentation-based appeal support for exchange account reviews
- On-chain evidence organization
- Case documentation for legal and compliance workflows
- Recovery support coordination
- Fraud prevention and risk awareness

## Case Material Handling

Sensitive case materials are handled under client authorization and controlled internal procedures.

We support documentation-based review, appeal, and recovery coordination workflows. We do not guarantee account reinstatement, fund release, asset recovery, or any outcome controlled by exchanges, platforms, counterparties, law enforcement agencies, or other third parties.

Delta&Capital (德尔泰) does not provide financial, investment, or legal advice through this GitHub organization. Public materials are provided for general informational and technical reference purposes only.
