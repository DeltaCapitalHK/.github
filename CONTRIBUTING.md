# Contributing

Delta Capital HK may publish selected public resources and lightweight tools related to digital asset incident response, blockchain forensic workflows, exchange account review preparation, and risk awareness.

Contributions should be:

- Factual and verifiable
- Free of private client information
- Free of confidential investigation material
- Clear about assumptions and limitations
- Respectful of legal, compliance, and privacy boundaries

Do not submit private keys, seed phrases, identity documents, exchange account materials, client files, or confidential case evidence.
